About Our Charity

Our mission at Ending Hunger Together is to end hunger by 2030 by implementing long-term programs that empower communities and providing assistance during and after natural catastrophes and times of conflict. Ending Hunger Together (EHT) is a major player in the global fight against hunger today. Our organization offers seamless synergy and support responsiveness in 50 countries, with an international footprint spanning all continents. Our top priorities are to testify about disadvantaged populations and take effective field measures. Our multi-sectoral approaches to addressing the various causes of hunger are founded in sound expertise.

Our Mission

Through the creation of social safety nets, market access for rural farmers, resilience to climate change, breaking the cycle of conflict and hunger, and the eradication of malnutrition, food waste, and loss, Ending Hunger Together (EHT) is dedicated to ensuring that wholesome food is available to people everywhere.

Our Vision

A world without hunger in which each person receives a wholesome plate of food at least three times a day.
