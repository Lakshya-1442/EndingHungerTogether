About Us

Our mission at Ending Hunger Together is to end hunger by 2030 by implementing long-term programs that empower communities and providing assistance during and after natural catastrophes and times of conflict. One of the main players in the global fight against hunger today is No to Hunger (NTH). Our organization offers seamless synergy and support responsiveness in 50 countries, with an international footprint spanning all continents. Our top priorities are to testify about disadvantaged populations and take effective field measures. Our multi-sectoral approaches to addressing the various causes of hunger are founded in sound expertise.
