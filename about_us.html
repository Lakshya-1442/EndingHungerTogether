We Believe in a World Without Hunger

  • - Where everyone has access to and the highest possible standard of living when it comes to food.

  • - Where mothers and the elderly can receive the nutrients they require in environments where children are born healthy and have a good start in life.

  • - Where families' access to food is unaffected by natural disasters and conflicts.

  • - Where farmers can grow enough food to feed themselves and participate in markets in areas where agriculture is at its best.

  • - Where, in times of food crises, all nations can participate and respond.

  • - Where communities everywhere collaborate to remove the circumstances that lead to chronic hunger.

  • - Where adequate food is available for everyone to realize their full potential.

Our Purpose

Through advocacy, fundraising, training and extension services, and participation in more than 50 countries across all continents, Ending Hunger Together (EHT) serves as a catalyst for community empowerment. We are committed to achieving Zero Hunger and help create sustainable food ecosystems for people all around the world.

Our Values

Our values are governed around collaborating with one another to reach our common goal of ensuring that every human being receives an adequate nutritious plate of food daily for them to reach their full potential.

How We Fight Hunger

Through policy leadership, fundraising and strategic outreach, we persuade communities, corporates, business entities and governments to support No To Hunger initiatives and join our movement to ensure food is available to all.

Policy

We design and develop policies that deliver a comprehensive approach to safeguarding food security and ensuring the eradication of hunger.

Grants & Funding

We consult, negotiate and collaborate with governments and likeminded organizations to secure financial commitment towards eradicating hunger including partnership with organizations such as the UN World Food programme (WFP)

Fundraising

We provide a direct channel for individuals, corporations and individuals to get involved through financial donations, community engagement and capacity building; including technical assistance to support our operations.

Capacity Building

We design, integrate and execute a broad range of activities in the agricultural sector that promote, enhance, and protect the food ecosystem to empower the communities and improve their quality of life.

About Us

Our mission at Ending Hunger Together is to end hunger by 2030 by implementing long-term programs that empower communities and providing assistance during and after natural catastrophes and times of conflict.

One of the main players in the global fight against hunger today is Ending Hunger Together (EHT). Our organization offers seamless synergy and support responsiveness in 50 countries, with an international footprint spanning all continents. Our top priorities are to testify about disadvantaged populations and take effective field measures. Our multi-sectoral approaches to addressing the various causes of hunger are founded in sound expertise.
